How to use Taransfer!

Send files and receive files with Taransfer! Here's how to start:

Uploading

Step 1

Login

First login with a Google account. Or with your phone number, which will send you a verification code you can enter in the "Enter verification code" textbox and you should be able to login successfully!

Step 2

Upload your file

Click the "browse..." button and select the file of your choosing to be uploaded.

Step 3

Enter email and Send!

After selecting a file, enter the receiver's email on the textbox and click "Tara! Send It!" A unique code will also be shown on screen and the same unique code will be sent to their email.
